Our Group of Companies (AES) has made environmental projects a priority for many years, but recent events have convinced us that we need a direct conduit from our own operators and suppliers. 

We also need better communication with our own staff, especially those with the vision and the expertise to see how climate-friendly change can also be business-friendly.

The Group has now decided to debate and publish a policy to prevent global warming through investment decisions and technological change. 

We can only act on what we know. And hopefully, once we know, we can work together to engineer a better world.
